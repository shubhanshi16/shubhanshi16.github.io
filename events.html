IEEE Day






IEEE Day commemorates the first time in history when engineers worldwide and IEEE members gathered to share their technical ideas in 1884. The celebration of IEEE Day on 1 October 2019 was the 10th year celebrating this historic event and marks one year to the culmination of the Realize the Full Potential of IEEE Campaign.
